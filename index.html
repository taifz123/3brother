About Us

Your Trusted Tobacco Shop

Three Brothers Tobacco Shop is Randwick's premier destination for premium tobacco products. Located in the heart of Randwick at 96 Belmore Road, we specialize in offering the finest selection of Cuban cigars, Dominican cigars, Nicaraguan cigars, and artisan pipe tobacco.

Our tobacco store features an extensive collection of premium cigars from renowned brands worldwide. Whether you're seeking authentic Cuban cigars like Cohiba and Montecristo, premium Dominican cigars such as Arturo Fuente and Davidoff, or bold Nicaraguan cigars including Padron and Oliva, we have something for every cigar enthusiast.

We also carry a wide range of artisan pipe tobacco blends, from traditional English mixtures to aromatic flavored tobacco, all carefully selected for quality and flavor. Our luxury smoking accessories include premium humidors, artisan cutters, and exclusive lighters.

Whether you're a seasoned cigar aficionado or new to pipe smoking, our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you through our curated selection. We pride ourselves on offering authentic products, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tobacco shop and premium products

What types of premium cigars do you sell?

We offer a comprehensive selection of premium cigars including authentic Cuban cigars (Cohiba, Montecristo, Romeo y Julieta), premium Dominican cigars (Arturo Fuente, Davidoff, Ashton), and bold Nicaraguan cigars (Padron, Oliva, Drew Estate). Our collection features limited edition releases and specialty blends for collectors and enthusiasts.

Do you carry artisan pipe tobacco?

Yes! We carry an extensive range of artisan pipe tobacco including traditional English blends, aromatic tobacco, Virginia tobacco, Burley tobacco, and our specialty house blends. Whether you prefer traditional English mixtures or aromatic flavored tobacco, we have options for every pipe smoker's taste, all handcrafted for exceptional quality.

What luxury smoking accessories do you stock?

Our tobacco shop carries a complete range of luxury smoking accessories including premium humidors, artisan cigar cutters, exclusive lighters, luxury pipes, premium ashtrays, and storage solutions. We stock both budget-friendly options and luxury accessories from renowned brands, all designed to enhance your smoking experience.

Are your premium tobacco products authentic?

Absolutely! All our premium tobacco products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers. We guarantee the authenticity of every cigar, tobacco product, and accessory we sell. As a licensed tobacco dealer in NSW, we maintain the highest standards of quality and authenticity for all our products.

What are your store hours and location?

Three Brothers Tobacco Shop is open 7 days a week from 8:00 AM to 10:30 PM, including public holidays. We're conveniently located at 96 Belmore Road, Randwick NSW 2031, just a 5-minute walk from Randwick Bus Interchange. For any inquiries about our premium products, call us at. Street parking is available on Belmore Road.

Do you offer expert advice for beginners?

Yes! Our experienced staff is always happy to help beginners choose the right premium cigars, artisan pipe tobacco, or luxury smoking accessories. Whether you're new to cigar smoking or pipe smoking, we'll guide you through our curated selection and help you find products that match your preferences and budget. Visit our Randwick store for personalized recommendations from our tobacco experts.
