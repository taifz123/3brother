About Three Brothers Tobacco Shop

Three Brothers Tobacco Shop has been Randwick's premier destination for premium tobacco products since 1985. Located in the heart of Randwick at 96 Belmore Road, we've been serving Sydney's tobacco enthusiasts for over 40 years.

Our tobacco store specializes in premium cigars, pipe tobacco, cigarettes, and a comprehensive range of smoking accessories. Whether you're a seasoned cigar aficionado or new to pipe smoking, our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you through our extensive selection.

We pride ourselves on offering authentic products from the world's finest tobacco manufacturers. Our cigar collection includes Cuban cigars, Dominican cigars, Nicaraguan cigars, and limited edition releases. Our pipe tobacco selection features English blends, aromatic tobacco, and specialty house blends.

As a family-owned tobacco shop in Randwick, we understand the importance of quality, authenticity, and customer service. Visit us today and experience why we're Sydney's most trusted tobacco dealer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tobacco shop and products

What types of cigars do you sell?

We offer a comprehensive selection of premium cigars including Cuban cigars, Dominican cigars, Nicaraguan cigars, and Honduran cigars. Our collection features popular brands like Cohiba, Montecristo, Romeo y Julieta, Arturo Fuente, Padron, and many more. We also stock limited edition cigars and specialty blends for collectors and enthusiasts.

Do you have pipe tobacco?

Yes! We carry an extensive range of pipe tobacco including English blends, aromatic tobacco, Virginia tobacco, Burley tobacco, and specialty house blends. Whether you prefer traditional English mixtures or aromatic flavored tobacco, we have options for every pipe smoker's taste.

What smoking accessories do you stock?

Our tobacco shop carries a complete range of smoking accessories including cigar cutters, cigar lighters, humidors, cigar cases, pipes, pipe cleaners, ashtrays, rolling papers, filters, rolling machines, and more. We stock both budget-friendly options and premium luxury accessories.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely! All our tobacco products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers. We guarantee the authenticity of every cigar, tobacco product, and accessory we sell. As a licensed tobacco dealer in NSW, we maintain the highest standards of quality and authenticity.

What are your store hours?

Three Brothers Tobacco Shop is open 7 days a week from 8:00 AM to 10:30 PM, including public holidays. We're conveniently located at 96 Belmore Road, Randwick NSW 2031. For any inquiries, call us at 0405 775 232.

Do you offer advice for beginners?

Yes! Our experienced staff is always happy to help beginners choose the right cigars, pipe tobacco, or smoking accessories. Whether you're new to cigar smoking or pipe smoking, we'll guide you through our selection and help you find products that match your preferences and budget. Visit our store for personalized recommendations.

Is parking available?

Yes, street parking is available on Belmore Road and surrounding streets. Our tobacco shop is also easily accessible by public transport - we're just a 5-minute walk from Randwick Bus Interchange and close to UNSW and Prince of Wales Hospital.
